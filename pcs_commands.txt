-->Here are the main components of a PCS (Pacemaker) cluster:

Nodes – The servers or machines in the cluster.

Pacemaker – The cluster resource manager that controls resources and failover.

Corosync – The messaging layer that handles communication and membership between nodes.

Resources – The services or applications managed by the cluster.

Fencing (STONITH) – The mechanism to isolate or reboot failed nodes to protect cluster integrity.
